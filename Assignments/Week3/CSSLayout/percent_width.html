Percent Width

Percent is a measurement unit relative to the containing block. It's great for images: here we make an image that is always 50% the width of its container. The whole section is set to 75% with a right float. As you resize the page, the image and section get smaller or larger at the percentage that is set.

Although you can use percentages for layout, it can get trickier and can have some unwanted results.
